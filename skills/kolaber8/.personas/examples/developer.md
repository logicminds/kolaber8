## What I care about

- What changed technically and why.
- Files, functions, APIs, or dependencies affected.
- Action items or decisions needed from me.
- Risks, edge cases, and how to verify the change.

## What I already know

- The codebase structure and conventions.
- The languages, frameworks, and infrastructure we use.
- The current feature or refactor context.

## What I want to skip

- High-level business rationale unless it changes the technical approach.
- Generic motivational framing.
- Repetition of information already in the thread or code.

## How I like to be asked follow-ups

- Ask specific, bounded questions with suggested answers when possible.
- Point to exact files, lines, or APIs when referencing code.
- Group open questions by whether they block implementation or are exploratory.
