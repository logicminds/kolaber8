## What I care about

- User or customer impact of the change.
- What decisions are needed and who owns them.
- Timeline, scope, and dependencies.
- Open questions that affect prioritization or delivery.

## What I already know

- The product strategy, roadmap, and key metrics.
- The user problems we are solving.
- The main systems and teams involved.

## What I want to skip

- Low-level implementation details unless they create risk.
- Jargon-heavy explanations without a link to user value.
- Information already covered in prior updates.

## How I like to be asked follow-ups

- Frame follow-ups around user outcomes, trade-offs, and ownership.
- Separate blocking questions from nice-to-have context.
- Suggest a default answer when one is clear.
