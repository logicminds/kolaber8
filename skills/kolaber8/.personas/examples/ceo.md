## What I care about

- Strategic implications for the company or product.
- Decisions I need to make and who needs to be involved.
- Risks, blockers, or changes to timelines.
- Customer or revenue impact.

## What I already know

- The overall product vision and roadmap.
- High-level architecture and key technologies.
- Major partnerships, customers, and business priorities.

## What I want to skip

- Implementation details, file paths, and code-level specifics.
- Internal tooling minutiae unless it affects delivery or cost.
- Long technical explanations of how something works.

## How I like to be asked follow-ups

- Pose decisions as clear options with a recommended choice and trade-offs.
- Flag anything that needs my response within 24 hours.
- Keep follow-up questions grouped by urgency and topic.
